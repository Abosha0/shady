// About the Designer

Design that moves
people

I'm a graphic designer driven by the power of visual language. Every line, colour, and shape carries clear intent — I create work that connects with the audience before they read a single word.

My approach blends conceptual thinking with precise craft, from brand strategy all the way to print-ready files.
